UI/UX Works

Travel Canva Case Study

As a travel app, Travel Canva provides an integrated interface that pops out destination options for travelers. After choosing the destinations, it will generate a route and make a travel planner for users, and the planner helps people to view all itineraries. Travel Canva also provides real-time tips from locals and experienced travelers for people to reference.

Looper App Design

The pandemic has exacerbated the need for users to have access to hygienic, accessible and inclusive public restrooms. LOOPER is an app that allows users to filter through and locate the nearest public restroom that meets their needs.

Government Agency Redesign

The current Brazilian Consulate website has a lot of errors in organizing and presenting the consulate information. The goal is to develop a user-friendly website that helps people to find consulate services faster and to locate information more accurately and efficiently.
